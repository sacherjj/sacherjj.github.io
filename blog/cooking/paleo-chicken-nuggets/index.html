Paleo Chicken Nuggets

Ingredients

  • 1 pound ground chicken
  • 1 egg yolk (see Herb Crackers for uses of egg white)
  • 1 teaspoon onion powder
  • 1/4 teaspoon garlic powder
  • 1/4 teaspoon paprika
  • 1/4 teaspoon sea salt + dash
  • 1/4 teaspoon fresh ground black pepper + dash
  • 1/2 cup + 1/3 cup almond flour
  • 1/2 cup unsweetened shredded coconut
  • 1/2 cup coconut oil

Supplies/Equipment

  • frying pan
  • two mixing bowls
  • parchment covered baking pan

Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 375 degrees.
  2. In a bowl one, mix to combine 1/4 cup almond flour, 1/2 cup coconut and salt & pepper.
  3. In a bowl two, combine:
  • ground chicken
  • 1/3 cup almond flour
  • onion powder
  • garlic powder
  • paprika
  • sea salt to taste
  • pepper to taste
  • egg yolk
  1. Mix well until incorporated.
  2. Melt coconut oil in frying pan on medium heat.
  3. Make nuggets:
  • Roll 2 tablespoons of the chicken mixture into ball
  • Coat with the coconut and almond mixture
  • Press slightly flat and add to pan
  1. Cook small batches for 3 to 4 minutes per side (don’t crowd the pan)
  2. Transfer nuggets to parchment lined bake pan
  3. Place in oven for 4 to 5 minutes to allow the chicken to cook through, as you fry the next batch.

Serve with Paleo “BBQ” Sauce

Saving Leftovers

Keep in fridge for a few days, but will loose the crunch.

Modifications

Eliminate coconut and replace with coarse almond flour.

Verdict

Not yet made with coconut. Can’t seem to make a recipe as printed, even the first time. :)
